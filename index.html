About Jerusalem

Jerusalem College of Engineering was established in the year 1995 under the umbrella of Jerusalem Education Trust to impart quality engineering education. Over the period of 23 years it has made a mark of itself and got established as an institution which offers excellent engineering education. The Institute has very good infrastructural facilities and laboratories with state-of-the art equipment. The institute is accredited by NAAC and NBA.

Rules

• Each team should contain 4 members . • One of them wants to flip the reusable or plastic bottle and rest of the members wants to play inside the game . • This game is similar to XO where the game requires the physical play of the players. • It is in 3×3 Matrix box . • While your team mate filps the bottle and it's stands upright inside the given square , then one of your team mate from the three should get to choose the place where they are going to place the player ( either in "X" or "O") and the opposite team should not dispute their choice. • Similarly the play will occur. • If the bottle didn't place properly then the next team will filp the bottle and this process will be happen vice versa condition. • The objective of this game is to form a straight line horizontally or vertically or diagonally within the square (either "X" or "O"). • The team which makes the straight line horizontally, vertically or diagonally will considered as the winners.
